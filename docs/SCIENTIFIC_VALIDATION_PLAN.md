# Scientific Validation Plan

The forward validation program and the exact claim ceiling for each resource.
What has been *done* is in [`FINDINGS.md`](FINDINGS.md) and
[`VALIDATION_REPORT.md`](VALIDATION_REPORT.md); this document is what remains and
the boundary each future result must respect. Data provenance (accessions,
SHA-256, licenses, exact object keys) is in [`../data/README.md`](../data/README.md);
canonical numbers are in [`results/findings.json`](../results/findings.json).

---

## 1. Corrections established during adversarial review

These are settled and must not regress:

- **Rank on the noise-aware verdict, never the raw `unreachable_fraction`** — it
  is magnitude-confounded (Spearman −0.56 Norman, −0.82 CaRPool, −0.69 Replogle).
- **Two-bar reporting is mandatory** and the bars are different tests: bar (a)
  `p<0.05`, bar (b) `floor_ratio ≥ 1.9×`. Never merge them into one significance
  label.
- **Threshold counts near the 1.9× bar are jittery** (Norman bar-b 35 vs 40
  across pipeline configs); report the z-distribution and ordering, not hard
  tallies.
- **"Unreachable" is model-relative**; every result object carries the `scope`
  disclaimer.
- **The Norman file provenance discrepancy** (file label `A549`; canonical Norman
  2019 is K562) is carried through every result; no cell-line claim beyond what
  the file states.
- **Order-3 is synthetic-only** — a machinery validation, never biological 3-way
  epistasis evidence.
- **The retired 60-shuffle / multisplit provenance** must never be presented as
  current inference.

## 3. Execution program

### Workstream 0 — software and lineage certification (ongoing)
Keep `scripts/validate_findings.py`, `results/manifest.json`, and `reproduce.sh`
in lockstep with `results/findings.json`. Every claim-bearing table is
regenerated by its runner, never copied from a notebook. Fail closed on status,
canonical values, paths, hashes, and executable bits.

### Workstream 1 — combinatorial certification (MAIN, delivered; extend)
Delivered: noise-aware two-bar certificate, synergy recovery, head-to-head,
prospective triage, k-way, cross-screen transfer, screenloop library
augmentation, acquisition recommender. **Next:** a same-experiment,
guide-burden- and control-matched set of *measured* in-domain combinations to
move certified emergence from retrospective to in-domain; an order-3 benchmark on
a screen that actually measures triples.

### Workstream 2 — leakage-safe transfer and structured inference
Return source-transfer validation to the independent Ota and Höllbacker inputs
(no coordinate selection using the held-out source). Add leakage-safe pseudobulk
donor- and guide-held-out evaluation; module/pathway holdouts; calibrated
structured nulls; nested PCA / ridge / unconstrained / capacity-matched
baselines. Resolve the exact guide rank-to-sgRNA-ID crosswalk (physical IDs are
absent from the ranked H5MU) before any physical-guide generalization claim.

### Workstream 3 — source-selected arrayed RNA/cytokine follow-up (delivered)
Zhu arrayed follow-up is complete (9 perturbations, 6 donor labels, bulk-RNA
top-1 1.0, IL-10/IL-21 flow 0.72–0.85). Ceiling: same-study cross-platform
follow-up, not held-out discovery, donor-population inference, or state
conversion.

### Workstream 4 — combination and modality stress tests (delivered, negative)
Goudy triple and guide-position transfer are bounded negatives, retained as
honest limits. **Next:** a non-confounded triple (single-vs-triple not entangled
with experiment/control/guide-burden) would convert the Goudy negative into a
real order-3 biological test.

### Workstream 5 — prospective validation (not started)
The highest-value open axis: convert the retrospective 2.4× triage enrichment
into a **forward-validated hit rate** on a screen run after the prediction.
Requires wet-lab partnership; no prospective or efficacy claim exists today.

## 6. Claim ceiling (the hard boundary, per capability)

- **Combinatorial emergence** — model-relative unreachability from the supplied
  single-gene cone above a stated measurement-noise model; **not** biological
  impossibility, validated synergy, target validation, dosing, or efficacy.
- **Prospective triage** — a modest rank enrichment (2.4× raw / 1.4× noise-robust
  on one screen), retrospective; **not** a solved prediction or a forward hit
  rate.
- **Cross-screen** — the *certificate* generalizes to CaRPool; the *triage's*
  magnitude-controlled component does not (needs per-screen recalibration).
- **Order-3** — synthetic code-path validation only.
- **Head-to-head** — the conceptual claim (predictors emit no certificate
  regardless of accuracy) is the point; the numeric MLP-vs-additive tie
  (0.896/0.897) is a bonus, not a claim to tie the foundation models.
- **Proving-ground / stress** — constrained directional / source-selected
  replication evidence; **not** predictive superiority, donor-population
  inference, or state conversion.

Every finding must state its ceiling inline. When in doubt, under-claim.
